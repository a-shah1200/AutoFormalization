	The final answer is $18x^{8}$".
   <\Natural language version>

2) <Natural language version>
 	"For integers n, let \[f(n)=\begin{cases} n^{2} & \text{if } n \text{ is odd,} \\ n^{2}-4n-1 & \text{if } n \text{ is even.} \end{cases}\]
	Find $f(f(f(f(f(4)))))$. The final answer is 1".
   <\Natural language version>
   <Isabelle Version>  
	theorem
             fixes f :: "int \<Rightarrow> int"
             assumes "\<forall>n. odd n \<longrightarrow> f n = n^2"
               and "\<forall> n. even n \<longrightarrow> f n = n^2 - 4*n - 1"
             shows "f 4 = -1" 
   <\Isabelle Version>

	The area of the base of a cone is 30 square units, and its height is 6.5 units. 
	What is the number of cubic units in its volume? The final answer is 65"

   <Isabelle Version>

	fixes b h v ::real

		and "h = 13 / 2"
	shows "v = 65"
